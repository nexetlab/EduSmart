Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

Where can I find my certificates?

All your completed certificates are available in the Certificates section. You can download them as PDF files or share them directly with employers via a unique link.

What are the system requirements?

EduSmart works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on both Windows and macOS. For mobile access, we recommend using our iOS or Android app for the best experience.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription in the Account Settings page. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period, and you'll retain access until that date.
